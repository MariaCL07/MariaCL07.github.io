Nubes de algodón (By Paulita)

Me desnudo ante mi misma. Dejo mi alma al descubierto; no quiero seguir en el camino que conlleva al abismo. Aquella luz verde me salvó, de esa oscuridad amarga. Mas amarga que la misma muerte sin precedentes.

Learn more

Some of us are shy, reserved and even lonely. This can be a barrier to sharing what you have inside you. Others have not had the opportunity to find what they are looking for. Some types of contacts can be scary aswell. And in the search for a solution to that "problem", I came up with the idea of creating a platform that really supports poets and writers who feel alone in the wonderful world of writing. Why not connect with souls who share the same passion? The cyber world has infinite things to offer and contribute to the real world. Why not make use of this wonder. For example, created a platform that brings trust and security, when it comes to opening your soul is crucial. A community that makes you grow and evolve is really beautiful! Writing is one of the most beautiful arts, so let's make something great out of it and out of us.
